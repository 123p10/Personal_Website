Eve Repository

I competed at the PCHack day hackathon May 18th, 2018 with three other teammates. We came second place at the hackathon with our idea "Eve".
Eve was a system developed in PHP utilizing the Twilio API. Eve allowed users to access the internet without standard connection to the internet. Eve was developed to assist people in remote areas or those who cannot afford data conenctions to access the internet. Rather then relying on Wifi or standard data connections, eve used the SMS protocol and sent messages to a server to process the request. The hackathon was relatively short, less then 6 hours was provided for development so we were only able to implement a google maps service, wikipedia searching and a news service.
