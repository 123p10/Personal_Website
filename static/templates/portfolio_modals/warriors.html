4659 Warrior Robotics

September 2016 - May 2018

I joined the Vex Robotics team at my school in Grade 10. I joined as one of the few programmers on the team. My primary role on this team was autonomous mode development. We competed in numerous competitions and were able to qualify to the Vex World Championships in Kentucky in 2017 through the design award. It was an amazing experience to compete at the world championships in my first year.
