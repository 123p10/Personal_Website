Self Driving Go Kart

May 2019 - August 2019

Go Kart Report

In May of 2019, I decided to try and build a self driving go kart with two other friends. We bought a gokart and modified it to be drive by wire. We also designed self driving software for the kart as shown in the report above. We had to abandon the project in September for numerous reasons, the primary reason being that we were all moving onto University, and the project was also financially infeasible, the hardware to run the self driving program would have required in excess of $1000 and we could not afford to continue development at the time.
